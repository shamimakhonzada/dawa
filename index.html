About Dawa Capital

Dawa is a trusted platform for private equity investments.

We bring you top private equity opportunities, handpicked from thousands of options — only about 5% make it onto our platform.

Each year we offer 20–30 opportunities, including direct deals, making it simple to spread investments across asset types, industries and regions.

Our Solutions

Private Capital Solutions

For qualified issuers seeking growth capital, Dawa Capital facilitates institutional-grade private placements through:

Equity Financing

  • Growth-stage investments: Raise capital by offering preferred/common equity to accredited investors
  • SCA-compliant structures: All offerings adhere to UAE Securities and Commodities Authority private placement regulations
  • Investor network: Access to family offices, venture capital firms, and institutional investors

Institutional Financing Solutions

For qualified businesses and growth-stage companies, Dawa Capital provides access to structured private credit solutions:

1. Private Debt Placements

  • Senior secured loans (AED 5M+ ticket sizes)
  • Venture debt facilities for scaling healthtech/MedTech companies
  • Sharia-compliant financing options available

2. Key Features

  • Exclusive to accredited borrowers and institutional counterparties
  • Competitive terms negotiated through our banking partnerships

Strategic Capital for Impact-Driven Ventures

Dawa Capital facilitates mission-aligned private placements for enterprises demonstrating both financial viability and measurable social impact.

For Qualified Issuers:

  • Impact Investment Vehicles: Structure private offerings that combine financial returns with ESG outcomes
  • Accredited Investor Network: Access capital from institutional investors and family offices focused on:
    • Healthcare innovation
    • Sustainable infrastructure
    • Education technology

Strategic Funding Solutions

For qualified enterprises, Dawa Capital provides access to non-dilutive capital opportunities through:

Government & Institutional Grant Advisory

We assist growth-stage companies in securing:

  • UAE-based grants: Matching clients with federal and emirate-level innovation programs
  • Global funding access: Strategic applications to EU Horizon, GCC development funds
  • Grant-to-equity bridges: Structured solutions for post-grant scaling capital

Eligibility Focus Areas:

  • Healthtech/biotech innovation
  • Industrial Revolution technologies
